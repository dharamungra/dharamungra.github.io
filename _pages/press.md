  <h3 class="research-card__title">
    <a href="https://techglobalinstitute.com/research/how-social-media-influencers-are-scaling-surrogate-political-campaigns-in-global-majority-elections/">How social media influencers are scaling surrogate political campaigns in Global Majority elections</a>
  </h3>
  <p class="research-card__meta">With Tech Global Institute</p>
  <ul class="research-card__tags">
    <li>India</li><li>Bangladesh</li><li>Indonesia</li><li>Philippines</li><li>Kenya</li><li>Election Integrity</li>
  </ul>
  <p class="research-card__body">
    Investigates content creators as paid political surrogates in Global Majority elections. Finds <strong>influencer-generated political content drives 50%+ higher engagement than non-political content</strong>, with creators often coerced into endorsements and aimed at hyper-local audiences. Exposes a policy gap: platforms govern parties and politicians, but rarely the sponsored political content of creators.
  </p>
