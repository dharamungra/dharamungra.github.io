<h1 style="margin-bottom: 0.2em;">Hi, I&rsquo;m Dhara.</h1>

<p class="hero-hook">On a mission to run fast &mdash; toward better products at <a href="https://simppl.org/">SimPPL</a>, and toward whatever the next race turns out to be.</p>

I am the co-founder and product lead at **[SimPPL](https://simppl.org/)**, a research collective building open-access AI tools for newsrooms, fact-checkers, non-profits, and civil society organizations in the global majority. Since February 2026, I have also been the inaugural **Gustav Struve Fellow in Global Responsible AI** at the **[University of Mannheim](https://www.uni-mannheim.de/)**, where I teach an *AI Product Development* workshop focused on practical industry applications and ethical design. I am a community member at the **[Tech Global Institute](https://techglobalinstitute.com/members/dhara-mungra/)**, where I have co-authored research on networked political influence and platform accountability across the Global Majority.

Earlier, I was co-founder and CTO at **[Sakhi](https://sakhi.simppl.org/)** (Jul 2024 &ndash; Dec 2025), an MIT- and UNICEF-incubated maternal health literacy venture; a data scientist at **[Bombora](https://bombora.com/)** building the Identity Graph (2021&ndash;2024); and one of the original developers of **[Gumshoe](https://www.gumshoex.org/)** at MuckRock &mdash; an NLP tool that helps journalists comb through FOIA email corpora. I completed my MS in Data Science at New York University, where I worked closely with professors and researchers who are now leading research initiatives at **Anthropic**.

I was also selected as a research group member at the **[Center for AI and Digital Policy](https://www.caidp.org/)**, contributing to the 2026 CAIDP Index through a country-level assessment of Malta&rsquo;s AI policy, and participated in the **[Global Changemakers](https://www.global-changemakers.net/) Summit 2025**, collaborating with international leaders to prototype scalable solutions for underserved communities.

Mentorship sits at the core of all of this. I believe **education is the most reliable leveling ground** &mdash; a belief shaped early, when I volunteered with **[Yuva Unstoppable](https://yuvaunstoppable.org/)** (2017&ndash;2019) teaching Math, Science, and English to children from underprivileged backgrounds. Today, most of SimPPL&rsquo;s team comes from Tier-III universities in India, pursuing research careers in AI, ML, and the social sciences.

Outside of work, I am a black belt &mdash; I taught martial arts to kids and led self-defense workshops for women and girls at **[RKKF](https://www.instagram.com/rkkf_rkkf/)** (2018&ndash;2019). I have fenced, kickboxed, and run two half marathons and a full marathon. Tennis is probably next.
